Anesthesia App Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our Anesthesia app. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Patient Data: Medical data such as drug history, allergy information, vitals, and other inputs you provide within the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and performance data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Provide core app functionalities drug recommendations
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Ensure safety checks for dosage errors

3. Data Storage and Security

All medical and personal data is securely stored and encrypted. We use industry-standard security practices to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of data.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your data with third parties without your explicit consent, except when required by law or for medical safety purposes.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for professional use and not for individuals under the age of 18.
